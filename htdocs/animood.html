What can ANIMOOD do?
More information about the problem here


ANIMOOD allows registration for students and professors.

Students are able to evaluate professors, organizations, organization events and offices.

Students are able to see the evaluation summary after evaluating.

Professors are able to see the evaluation summary.

ANIMOOD also allows org heads to register a member in their organization.

ANIMOOD also allows org heads to appoint another admin in their organization.
